Enelly - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Enelly is an innovative NFT project launching on Linea Chain, inspired by GOAT but with its own unique vision, branding, and utility. It aims to bring real value to holders beyond just being a profile picture.

What makes Enelly unique?

  • •Strong Community & Ecosystem
  • •Real Utility for Holders
  • •Integration with the Linea Chain ecosystem.

What blockchain is Enelly on?

Enelly is launching on Linea Chain, known for its scalability, low fees, and security.

The official supply will be revealed soon. Stay tuned for details!

We're creating real utility for holders. Some potential benefits include:

More details will be announced soon!
